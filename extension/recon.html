Welcome to Reddit Tracker

A few things to know before you start:

  1. Connect the bridge first. Check the dot in the top bar. Green means the bridge is running and you are ready. If it is red, just ask Claude to start the Reddit Tracker bridge (or click Connect for the command to run yourself). Nothing works until that dot is green.
  2. Add your brand. Click + Add brand (under the Brand menu at the top) and enter the name, website, and a short description of what the brand is. That description matters: it is how the tool tells real mentions apart from unrelated things that happen to share the name.
  3. Scans run in your browser, and large ones are slow. Scanning opens real Google and Reddit pages with pauses in between so you do not get blocked. The more mentions there are, the longer it takes. A big scan can run for many minutes. You can press Stop at any time, and anything already found is saved.
  4. You solve CAPTCHAs, not the tool. Google sometimes shows a "confirm you're not a robot" check while scanning. When that happens the tool pauses and brings the tab to the front. Solve it and the scan continues on its own. The tool will not fill it in for you.
  5. Check Selectors tests the scraper. Google and Reddit occasionally change how their pages are built, which can quietly stop the tool from finding things. The Check Selectors button (top right) runs a quick test on live pages and tells you if anything is broken. Run it if your results suddenly come back empty.

This is your tool to shape. It runs entirely on your own machine, so the ideal way to use it is to tweak and iterate on it with your own copy of Claude Code as your needs change.
